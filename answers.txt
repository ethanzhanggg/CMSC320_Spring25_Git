Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: Git provides version control, facilitates collaboration, supports branching and merging, is a distributed system, and 
        integrates well with various development tools and platforms.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Data science can be applied to healthcare. 
        For example, predictive analytics can be used to forecast patient outcomes and improve treatment plans.

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging combines the contents of two branches, preserving the history of both. 
        Rebasing moves or combines a sequence of commits to a new base commit, creating a linear history.
